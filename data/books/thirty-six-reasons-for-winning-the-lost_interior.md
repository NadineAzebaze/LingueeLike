## Preface {.unnumbered}

The Lord Jesus had one reason for coming down from heaven into our
world. That reason was to seek and to save the lost. The Bible says,
*Here is a trustworthy saying that deserves full acceptance: Christ
Jesus came into the world to save sinners---of whom I am the worst* (1
Timothy 1:15). The Lord Jesus said, *For the Son of man came to seek and
to save what was lost* (Luke 19:10).

The Lord Jesus came to seek and to save the lost. He died on the cross
for all sinners and thus shed His blood for all men. Everyone who
believes in the Lord Jesus is saved from the consequence of his sin,
that is, eternal separation from God in hell. Everyone who does not
believe in the Lord Jesus will go to hell.

The one reason why the Lord Jesus did not take you to heaven the day you
believed was that you should stay on earth and be His partner in winning
the lost.

In this book we have given you reasons why you must win the lost. May
the Holy Spirit set your heart on fire as you read the book, so that you
will take no rest but give yourself wholeheartedly to the supreme task
of winning the lost.

## The first set of reasons for winning the lost

### Because the gospel is the power of God

*I am not ashamed of the gospel, because it is the power of God for the
salvation of everyone who believes: first for the Jew, then for the
Gentile. For in the gospel a righteousness from God is revealed, a
righteousness that is by faith from first to last, just as it is
written: "The righteous will live by faith"* (Romans 1:16‒17).

Say with me: "I am not ashamed of the gospel, because it is the power of
God unto salvation for whoever believes." The gospel is more powerful
than unbelief. The gospel is more powerful than Islam, Hinduism,
sorcery, atheism, youth, old age, materialism, love of sin, love of the
world, love of self, persecutors.

Present the gospel to each of these and they will be conquered. Present
it to the Moslem and he will believe. Present it to the Hindu and he
will be conquered. Present it to sorcerers and they will believe.
Present it to atheists and they will believe. Present it to the old and
they will believe. Present it to the youth and they will be conquered to
love the Lord. Present it to the old and they will believe. Present it
to the materialistic and they will love Jesus. Present it to persecutors
and they will be converted.

The gospel is the message of the Lion of the tribe of Judah and the lion
does not turn back. Do not turn back. Do not exclude anybody. Watchman
Nee said he preached the gospel to someone who did not believe in God,
in resurrection and so on. He preached everything. The man did not
believe and one week later the man was in the faith.

The command is to go and preach the gospel to all creation. It means all
creation can believe.

The gospel is ultimate power. The gospel is irresistible. If there is a
club of sorcerers, that will make a good place to evangelise. It will be
taking power to those needing power. Do not protect the gospel; preach
it. Do not protect a lion, release it. Other things need to be
protected, not the gospel. Preach it.

We have talked about this and that class that is difficult. No one needs
to believe anything in order to qualify to hear the gospel. We start
with those who believe nothing. Or we start with those who believe
everything except the gospel.

Do not tell us that your people are hard. The gospel is harder. Do not
tell us that your people are impossible. The gospel is more impossible.
Do not tell us that your people are resistant. The gospel is more
resistant. The gospel is might. The gospel is glory. The gospel is God's
creation. It is backed by heaven. The Triune God is behind the gospel.
If you are in the gospel, you are in the Triune God.

### The one who wins souls is wise

*The fruit of the righteous is a tree of life, and he who wins souls is
wise* (Proverbs 11:30). He who does not win souls is foolish. He who
wins souls is wise. Do you want to be among the wise? Win souls and you
are immediately among the wise. I who win souls, am wise. We who win
souls are wise. Investment in soul-winning is investment in wisdom. May
we all be wise by soul-winning!

Let every young believer be indoctrinated in this wisdom. If a person
does a forty-day fast, we acclaim them. It is as if he should walk on
everyone. We have made fasting the epitome activity of our ministry. It
is wrong. The goal is not to fast. The goal is souls. Our goal is one
billion people. The method is the making of disciples. The price to be
paid is in holiness, prayer, fasting, giving, sacrifice, suffering, and
so on.

Where do we keep our eyes? On the goal. The hero produces souls. If you
can produce souls without prayer, produce them. If you can produce souls
without fasting, produce them. If you can produce souls without walking
with God, produce them. You pay the price so that you may have the
harvest.

Greatness is in souls won, not in the price paid. Henceforth, greatness
shall be measured by souls won and kept. If not, we shall have denied
the goal.

Someone goes fishing with twelve modern boats. He takes along engineers,
a specialist in cold storage, works twenty-four hours a day and catches
no fish. Then another fellow goes with a hook and a worm and, in a few
minutes, catches a huge fish. Who is the fisherman? The man who brings
home the fish.

Fishermen are known for catching fish, not for boats, engineers or
diplomas. Since our goal is in souls won and maintained, the great shall
be those who win abiding souls. May this be driven hard into our hearts!
It is by the fish caught that you know a fisherman.

Are you winning souls or are you involved in unproductive
activity---activity that hinders the winning of souls? Let us indulge in
activities that lead to soul-winning.

### Because the Lord commanded it

*Then he opened their minds so they could understand the Scriptures. He
told them, 'This is what is written: The Christ will suffer and rise
from the dead on the third day, and repentance and forgiveness of sins
will be preached in his name to all nations, beginning at Jerusalem. You
are witnesses of these things. I am going to send you what my Father has
promised; but stay in the city until you have been clothed with power
from on high* (Luke 24:45‒48).

### Because the Lord commanded it as his dying wish

Do you put aside a man's dying wish? *Then Jesus came to them and said,
"All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Therefore,
go and make disciples of all nations, baptising them in the name of the
Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey
everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the
very end of the age"* (Matthew 28:18‒20).

*He said to them, "You are the ones who justify yourselves in the eyes
of men, but God knows your hearts. What is highly valued among men is
detestable in God's sight. The Law and the Prophets were proclaimed
until John. Since that time, the good news of the kingdom of God is
being preached, and everyone is forcing his way into it"* (Luke
16:15‒16).

His dying wish was, "Go into all the world and preach the gospel to all
creation."

The people we logically think will not believe soon become leading
believers. Religious people are the most difficult to win to Christ.
They are so near religion, but so far from God. God and religion are
poles apart. A pagan is easier to win to Christ than a pseudo-Christian.
Do not exclude people. Do not exclude the rich. They are the most
desperate of people. You know the sins that are committed among them.

In one country, four diplomats from one industrialised country met to
exchange their wives by lot in order to have some fun. When you listen
to some of the sins of the upper class, they of all men are to be
pitied. Let us not neglect them. They are great seekers. It depends on
what they are seeking.

Some of them have gone and done very humiliating things. Some have
crawled on their stomachs to enter the workshops of sorcerers. Let us
not be put off by the fact that the man is cased in some metal called a
car. In one of the homes, I had to go up into the ceiling seven times
because charms were thought to have been hidden up there. A tree in the
compound was cut down because they said it was haunted. This could be
done because the wife of the home had believed. Let us give them the
gospel. May the Lord help us to spare no one the full dose of the
gospel!

### Because Jesus came for that purpose

*For the Son of Man came to seek and to save what was lost* (Luke
19:10). That is why Jesus came. We must win the lost because Jesus came
to seek and to save the lost. We are in the same business with Jesus if
we seek and save the lost.

### We win the lost in order to save ourselves from blood guilt

*Son of man, I have made you a watchman for the house of Israel; so hear
the word I speak and give them warning from me. When I say to a wicked
man, 'You will surely die,' and you do not warn him or speak out to
dissuade him from his evil ways in order to save his life, that wicked
man will die for his sin, and I will hold you accountable for his blood.
But if you do warn the wicked man and he does not turn from his
wickedness or from his evil ways, he will die for his sin; but you will
have saved yourself* (Ezekiel 3:17‒19).

*The word of the LORD came to me: Son of man, speak to your countrymen
and say to them: 'When I bring the sword against a land, and the people
of the land choose one of their men and make him their watchman, and he
sees the sword coming against the land and blows the trumpet to warn the
people, then if anyone hears the trumpet but does not take warning and
the sword comes and takes his life, his blood will be on his own head.
Since he heard the sound of the trumpet but did not take warning, his
blood will be on his own head. If he had taken warning, he would have
saved himself. But if the watchman sees the sword coming and does not
blow the trumpet to warn the people and the sword comes and takes the
life of one of them, that man will be taken away because of his sin, but
I will hold the watchman accountable for his blood.*'

*Son of man, I have made you a watchman for the house of Israel; so hear
the word I speak and give them warning from me. When I say to the
wicked, 'O wicked man, you will surely die,' and you do not speak out to
dissuade him from his ways, that wicked man will die for his sin, and I
will hold you accountable for his blood* (Ezekiel 33:1‒ 8)*.*

If we do not warn the lost, they will die, but the Lord will demand
their blood of us. How can we carry the blood of others on us? Think of
those to whom you did not preach. Think of those you preached to without
power, without prayer.

I am accountable for the blood of those who have not yet believed, in
all the nations.

The blood of all who could have been reached, who have not yet been
reached is upon me; the blood of all who could have been reached, who
were not reached is upon you.

Twenty-five thousand Indians die every day without hearing the gospel.
What have we done to reduce that number?

The blood of all who are lost without the gospel is our responsibility.
The blood of all who died all over the world without hearing the gospel
is upon us. The blood of all who died all over the world without hearing
the gospel is upon you. Their blood is upon you as an individual. It is
upon us as a people.

The blood of everyone who perishes in your quarter without hearing the
gospel is upon you.

The blood of everyone who perishes in your city without hearing the
gospel is upon you.

The blood of everyone who perishes in your country without hearing the
gospel is upon you.

The blood of everyone who perishes on your continent without hearing the
gospel is upon you.

The blood of everyone who perishes on planet Earth without hearing the
gospel is upon you.

You are under obligation whether you confess all these things or not. It
is not because you confess them that they become binding. They are
binding whether or not you confess them.

Commit yourself to actively: preach the gospel.

win the lost.

transform those won into disciples.

teach them to also go and make disciples.

Anyone who ought to go to the mission field, but who is still loitering
around, is amassing blood upon himself. The church that has not sent
missionaries to other nations is guilty of the blood of many.

In the first coming of the Lord, He gave Himself to the winning of the
lost. He did not take care of the social issues. He will come again.
When He does come, He will give himself to the socioeconomic issues.
Therefore, we have only one thing now---to win the lost. When He comes
back it will be impossible to win the lost.

Will you say on that day that you gave yourself to the right thing if
you allow the lost to perish because you wanted to be governor, improve
the roads, improve the educational system, and do other things? You will
still answer for the blood of those who perished.

There is only one thing to do now: preach the gospel and save the lost.
It is not that the other things are not important. If a boat is sinking,
and you can save both the men and the goods, do. But if it is not
possible to save the two, save the men.

The world is a sinking boat. Let us do all to save the lost in the
greatest number possible. There is sanity in releasing our leaders from
secular jobs, and giving them an allowance so that they preach the
gospel.

There are enough unbelievers to do the secular jobs. But Christian
leaders of worth are in short supply. If we are not a sick church, we
shall give our leaders salaries so that they may concentrate on saving
the lost.

Any pursuit of education beyond the minimum needed for the preaching of
the gospel is criminal. Two more years in the laboratory means that some
will perish without the gospel. What if a man goes to the mission field
five years late? Some people will perish and perish forever.

When a leader spends the weekend farming cocoyams instead of winning the
lost, he has lost his head. If he starts one or two businesses, he is a
traitor. The Lord will ask of him the blood of the perishing. Is this
right thinking? Whether you agree or not, the Scriptures stand.

There are some who must stop their secular jobs. Let them stop the
wasting of time and we shall give them a salary so that they put the
hours into the gospel.

For many of us it is already late evening. Should we flirt around
anymore? You may gain 10,000 more from a second job or business. But how
will you account for the lost souls of men?

Every minute put into some other thing is a minute spent away from
winning the lost. When Jesus comes, there will be the Jesus economic
system. Now, there is very little that can be done. One of the Secretary
Generals of the United Nations said, "We have tried very much, we have
failed woefully."

No child should be allowed to serve the world unless he can do nothing
at all for the gospel. Those who saw from the beginning gave their lives
exclusively to the gospel. They have lost nothing.

Ask yourself the question: "Why am I on earth? Should I invest one
second in building an accursed system ready for destruction?" Go to the
mission field as soon as possible, so that the blood of men may not rest
upon you.

A brother came from Nigeria for a course in Cameroon. The course was
postponed. He went on a seven-day fast and a retreat, after which he
took along a partner for daily evangelism. Together they led two men,
two women, and five children to the Lord. When he was disappointed, that
is what he did. To be disappointed by man only means that God has better
plans.

### Because of the reality of hell

The Bible says, *Then death and Hades were thrown into the lake of fire.
The lake of fire is the second death. If anyone's name was not found
written in the book of life, he was thrown into the lake of fire*
(Revelation 20:14‒15).

We further read, *As the weeds are pulled up and burned in the fire, so
it will be at the end of the age. The Son of Man will send out his
angels, and they will weed out of his kingdom everything that causes sin
and all who do evil. They will throw them into the fiery furnace, where
there will be weeping and gnashing of teeth* (Matthew 13:40‒42).

*There was a rich man who was dressed in purple and fine linen and lived
in luxury every day. At his gate was laid a beggar named Lazarus,
covered with sores and longing to eat what fell from the rich man's
table. Even the dogs came and licked his sores.*

*The time came when the beggar died and the angels carried him to
Abraham's side. The rich man also died and was buried. In hell, where he
was in torment, he looked up and saw Abraham far away, with Lazarus by
his side. So he called to him, 'Father Abraham, have pity on me and send
Lazarus to dip the tip of his finger in water and cool my tongue,
because I am in agony in this fire.' But Abraham replied, 'Son, remember
that in your lifetime you received your good things, while Lazarus
received bad things, but now he is comforted here and you are in agony.
​And besides all this, between us and you a great chasm has been fixed,
so that those who want to go from here to you cannot, nor can anyone
cross over from there to us.' He answered, 'Then I beg you, father, send
Lazarus to my father's house, for I have five brothers. Let him warn
them, so that they will not also come to this place of torment'* (Luke
16:19‒28).

Hell is real. You cannot take chances about the reality of it. You dare
not land there. You dare not allow anyone else to go there. Ensure that
no one whom you could save from hell ever gets there. Jesus was
certainly not joking when He talked about hell.

### Because of the horror of hell

In the passage quoted above, the rich man in question could say, *...
"Father Abraham, have pity on me and send Lazarus to dip the tip of his
finger in water and cool my tongue, because I am in agony in this fire."
.... "I beg you, father, send Lazarus to my father's house, for I have
five brothers. Let him warn them, so that they will not also come to
this place of torment"* (Luke 16:24‒28).

There will be such pain, untold horror and gnashing of teeth in hell.

Are you ready for such pain, horror and anguish? Will you for any
reason, let any other person, go to hell? Will you be justified if you
allowed anyone to go to hell because you were tired, hungry, indulgent,
discouraged, or wanted more money, or more luxury and so on? Do
something about it. Do something about it immediately.

### Because of the duration of hell

It will be unending, eternal, everlasting torment. After one billion
years, staying there will just have begun. It is the place of
everlasting pain, sorrow, and gnashing of teeth. If you get there, all
hope of ever getting out is lost. If you allow anyone to get there, all
hope of ever getting him out is lost and lost eternally.

Think about your family members who are not saved,

neighbours who are not saved,

friends who are not saved,

classmates who are not saved,

colleagues who are not saved,

those you meet in taxis who are not saved,

those you meet in offices who are not saved,

> those you meet along the road who are not saved,

those you buy from or sell to who are not saved,

many other people you meet.

Does it matter to you that they are on their way to eternal damnation
unless you personally do something about it? Will you do something about
it?

## The second set of reasons for winning the lost

### Because the devil will be personally in hell

If you go to hell, you will be in the devil's immediate presence. Just
the horror of what his demons cause is alarming. But in hell your
nearest neighbour will be the devil. You will be living in the presence
of distilled evil. Whatever will happen to the devil will happen to you.
Being in Satan's immediate presence! When you think about it, there
could be nothing more evil.

### Because of the reality of heaven

Heaven is real. We shall be there. Those we win to the Lord will be
there. The Bible says, *When the Son of Man comes in his glory, and all
the angels with him, he will sit on his throne in heavenly glory. All
the nations will be gathered before him, and he will separate the people
one from another as a shepherd separates the sheep from the goats. He
will put the sheep on his right and the goats on his left. Then the King
will say to those on his right, Come, you who are blessed by my Father;
take your inheritance, the kingdom prepared for you since the foundation
of the world (*Matthew 25:31‒34).

Jesus commanded us to store up treasures for ourselves in heaven:

*But store up for yourselves treasures in heaven, where moth and rust do
not destroy, and where thieves do not break in and steal (*Matthew
6:20).

He could not have been talking about an imaginary place.

### Because of the wondrous beauty and excellence of heaven

The Bible says,

One *of the seven angels who had the seven bowls full of the seven last
plagues came and said to me, "Come, I will show you the bride, the wife
of the Lamb." And he carried me away in the Spirit to the mountain great
and high, and showed me the Holy City, Jerusalem, coming down out of
heaven from God. It shone with the glory of God, and its brilliance was
like that of a very precious jewel, like a jasper, clear as crystal. It
had a great, high wall with twelve gates, and with twelve angels at the
gates. On the gates were written the names of the twelve tribes of
Israel. There were three gates on the east, three on the north, three on
the south, and three on the west. The wall of the city had twelve
foundations, and on them were the names of the twelve apostles of the
Lamb.*

*The angel who talked with me had a measuring rod of gold to measure the
city, its gates and its walls. The city was laid out like a square, as
long as it was wide. He measured the city with the rod and found it to
be 12,000 stadia in length, and as wide and high as it is long. He
measured its wall and it was 144 cubits thick, by man's measurement,
which the angel was using. The wall was made of jasper, and the city of
pure gold, as pure as glass. The foundations of the city walls were
decorated with every kind of precious stone. The first foundation was
jasper, the second sapphire, the third chalcedony, the fourth emerald,
the fifth sardonyx, the sixth carnelian, the seventh chrysolite, the
eighth beryl, the ninth topaz, the tenth chrysoprase, the eleventh
jacinth, and the twelfth amethyst. The twelve gates were twelve pearls,
each gate made of a single pearl. The great street of the city was made
of pure gold, like transparent glass.*

*I did not see a temple in the city, because the Lord God Almighty and
the Lamb are its temple. The city does not need the sun or the moon to
shine on it, for the glory of God gives it light, and the Lamb is its
lamp. The nations* *will walk by its light, and the kings of the earth
will bring their splendour into it. On no day will* *its gates ever be
shut, for there will be no night there. The glory and honour of the
nations will be brought into it. Nothing impure will ever enter it, nor
will anyone who does what is shameful or deceitful, but only those whose
names are written in the Lamb's book of life* (Revelation 21:9‒27).

Could anything be more glorious?

Surely, you cannot afford to miss it.

You must not let anyone miss it!

### Because of the stupendous duration of heaven

After we shall have been there ten billion years we shall just have
begun our stay there. Oh, the wonder of being there! There will be much
space there. It is massive. The city is 2,200 km long, 2,200 km wide and
2,200 km high.

That is one city! The city is a cube. Imagine it. Consider the
possibility of missing it.

Think about it: For two thousand years, Jesus has been putting the
finishing touches on the mansions there with all the angels working
non-stop. There is nothing on earth to compare with it. We must win
souls because what they will miss if we do not. will be too unimaginable
a thing!

### Because the Lord Jesus will be in heaven

He will be there. He will be the Lamp in heaven. We shall not need the
sun. The city does not need the sun or the moon to shine on it, for the

### Because man can be saved

The lost can be saved. How can I allow someone who can be saved to go to
hell? When the Lord gave the command to go and make disciples of all
nations, it was because He knew that lost man could be redeemed. When He
gave the Holy Spirit and His power, it was because man could be and had
to be saved. He himself came to seek and to save that which was lost. He
has left us to continue the seeking and the saving of that which was
lost.

We dare not allow those who could possibly be saved to perish. We dare
not discontinue His mission on earth. We dare not disobey His command.
Let us be busy in the business of soul-winning. The lost are being
saved. The lost shall be saved.

### Because hell was not created for man

Hell was created for the devil and his angels. Man was not created for
hell. We owe it to man to do all that is possible to block even those
who want to go to hell from going there. We shall ensure that those who
want to go to hell do not reach there. We must not just allow people to
have what they want. We must pray so that God melts hardened hearts, so
that those who hate heaven may begin to love it.

### Because the Holy Spirit was given for that purpose

The Bible says, *After his suffering, he showed himself to these men and
gave many convincing proofs that he was alive. He appeared to them over
a period of forty days and spoke about the kingdom of God. On one

The Holy Spirit was given to make you a witness in your town.

The Holy Spirit was given to make you a witness in your nation.

The Holy Spirit was given to make you a witness on your continent.

The Holy Spirit was given to make you a witness in your world.

### The law of sowing and reaping

God is extravagant in His law of sowing and reaping.

A woman releases nearly 500 ovules for the few children she may have.

A man releases between sixty million and one hundred million sperm cells
for one conception that may take place.

Countless mango flowers and those that do not mature are produced for
the mangoes that may be harvested.

The Lord Jesus reached out to many thousands for the one hundred and
twenty He had.

The one who sows bountifully will reap bountifully. The quantity of the
harvest is dependent on the quantity of the sowing. The quality of the
harvest is dependent on the quality of the sowing. The sowing is
determinant. The sowing and the harvesting are inseparable.

What is sown determines what will be reaped.

The seed that is sown and the harvest that is obtained cannot be
separated. They are one. One is a continuation of the other.

The best sown, the best harvested.

The worst sown, the worst harvested.

Abundant sowing, abundant harvesting.

Life should be measured by the souls won to the Lord Jesus. The Lord
Jesus came from heaven to be a soul-winner on earth. He came to seek and
to save that which was lost.

Of every 100 unbelievers who clearly hear the gospel, one can be
expected to become a member of a dynamic cross-bearing local church.

For an individual to complain about the hardness of a people when he has

The price to be paid must always be put side by side with the desired
results

## The third set of reasons for winning the lost

### Because of the joy in doing it

The winners of the lost are the happiest people on earth. They are the
instruments for bringing sinners to repentance. And the repentance of
one sinner causes heaven to rejoice. When heaven is rejoicing because a
sinner has repented, they are part of that joy. Then they also rejoice
for being instrumental in bringing about the joy of heaven. They are the
most fulfilled persons on planet Earth. They know a reward is awaiting
them.

No one can be busy winning souls and be sad. The lot of sad believers is
also the lot of those who are not involved in active soul-winning. They
know nothing of the jubilation of heaven over the repentance of a
sinner. They look forward to crowns for the lost won.

Go, win souls. Make heaven happy. Rejoice with heaven. And be filled
with the joy of your coming reward.

### Because it will hasten the return of the Lord Jesus whom we love

We want to see Him, we want to be with Him.

*And this gospel of the kingdom will be preached in the whole world as a
testimony to all nations, and then the end will come* (Matthew 24:14).

All the current speculations about the return of the Lord make no sense
while there are whole nations and peoples unreached with the gospel of
salvation. Such talk is a waste of time and will yield no fruit. God is
certainly not part of it. He cannot be part of the expression of
idleness at the cost of the perishing souls of men.

The lovers of the Lord are at one task now. They are winning the lost.
They want this gospel of the Kingdom to be preached in the whole world,
as a testimony to all nations, and then the end will come. And then the
Lord will come. That is the only way to hasten the return of the Lord
they so much love to be with. Be one of them. In this way we shall soon
be with our Lord.

### So that Jesus should have many brothers

When I was young, I wanted to have the twelve tribes of Zach Fomum. In
Uganda I saw a person who belongs to a family of twenty four from one
mother. But we shall not overpopulate the earth. If you have three
children, you have eliminated yourself from the mission field. The cost
of keeping you abroad will be too much. If you want to go to the mission
field, discipline your productivity.

Now, let us populate the world with the brothers of Jesus. Every new
convert is a new brother of the Lord Jesus. Actually, He came to seek
and to save the lost so that they may become His brothers. He died for
all so that all may become His brothers. It hurts Him to see anyone die
without that the bond of brotherhood with Him has been established
through conversion.

Go and win a lost person. Then come and tell Jesus, "I have brought
another brother for You." Pray that this incentive should spur us to win
the lost. Will you not give Jesus another brother?

### Because there is room in heaven for them

If God takes to heaven every child who dies at birth, and all who die
before they could be born, and all the believers, there will still be
enormous room for all of them and for many more people. Let us win them
because the space there is vast. There can never be a problem of space
in heaven for all who are in Christ.

### Because of the joy of populating heaven

God is interested in numbers. Let us make it our aim to populate heaven.
It is also a joy to know that one can and is actually adding to the
number of those who will be in heaven.

### Because of the joy of depopulating hell

We set out to depopulate hell to ensure that heaven is populated. When a
person is saved, hell has lost a candidate and heaven has gained one. If
some must, however, go to hell, let their number be as reduced as
possible.

*Yet it was the LORD's will to crush him and cause him to suffer, and
though the LORD makes his life a guilt offering, he will see his

his hand. After the suffering of his soul, he will see the light of life
and be satisfied; by his knowledge my righteous servant will justify

Multitudes beyond number are His offspring.

### Because that is the only reason why you are still on earth

The only reason why Jesus did not take me to heaven the day I believed
is because there were the lost to be won for Him. I must, therefore, be
at the one task for which He left me here on earth. You, too, must be at
the task for which He left you here on earth.

### Because the lord Jesus gave his all for that reason

He did not spare His glory in heaven. He did not spare His life here on
earth. He emptied Himself of everything so that the lost might be saved.
He could say, *... and I lay down my life for the sheep ... I lay it
down of my own accord* (John 10: 15, 18).

You and I cannot afford to be negligent about that for which the Lord
Jesus Christ gave His all in heaven and on earth. What shall we say to
Him on that day?

### The burden and passion for the lost

Burden follows the soul-winner to the place of soul-winning, but burden
will never precede the soul-winner before he has begun to win souls.

The soul-winner begins to win souls by simple obedience and, as he
continues to do so, the burden for souls will come upon him and grow as
he continues to win souls.

The burden will come upon those who act without burden. As they continue
to witness, the burden will grow until it possesses them and then
becomes permanent.

He who [perseveres]{.underline} to witness without burden will
eventually receive a burden for souls. The one who
[perseveres]{.underline} in witnessing with burden will have the burden
settled on him permanently. When the burden has been received,
witnessing must continue, and go on and on until the person is breaking.
Only in this way will the burden be retained.

Those who stop witnessing soon after the burden to witness has come upon
them, will lose the burden.

A passion for souls is created by the Holy Spirit in the believer. Where
He has absolute sway, He will fill the believer to overflowing, and that
one will overflow with a passion for souls

Passion for souls is an overflow of intimacy with the Lord.

A passion for souls is not something that anyone can have apart from
Jesus.

A passion for souls flows from union with Him.

A passion for the lost is the overflow of a rich and overflowing union
with God.

The fire of God in the believer is released as he begins to talk to an
unbeliever about the Lord Jesus.

Passion is directly proportional to the time spent in reaching out to
the lost.

Passion increases with the frequency with which souls are won to the
Lord.

Passion for souls increases with the number of people evangelised.

R*emember this: whoever sows sparingly will also reap sparingly, and
whoever sows generously will also reap generously* (2 Corinthians 9:6).

Passion for souls is given to those who invest their time bleedingly
into the salvation of the lost.

Passion is given to those who concentrate and persist until the soul is
won and established in Christ.

Passion for souls is translated into money invested into the battle for
souls.

There is an urgency factor in the matter of financial investments for
the salvation of the lost. It is a measure of how soon available funds
are converted into winning the souls of men. It is a measure of the
urgency with which what one possesses is transferred so that it becomes
the money of the treasury of God, and then how soon the money of the
treasury of God is converted into money used for souls, and the like.

There is a personal urgency factor. It spells out how soon the money I
give is given. This reflects how soon I want the lost to hear the gospel
and be saved. This reflects how soon I want missionaries to be trained
and sent out. This reflects how soon I want new areas to be conquered.

This personal urgency factor determines the impact of one's investments
into the gospel:

Money is such a serious matter before God that it touches the heart of
God in a most special way for blessing or for cursing.

Money is to accomplish only one purpose: save the lost. All other uses
are secondary; all other uses are tertiary; all other purposes can be
laid aside without great loss.

## The fourth set of reasons for winning the lost

### Because the Lord Jesus won the lost

He won the lost. Let us, as His followers, also win the lost. He has set
an example and a pattern to follow. We cannot do otherwise and yet be
His followers.

### Because the apostles won the lost

The Bible says, *Then Peter stood up with the Eleven, raised his voice
and addressed the crowd: Fellow Jews and all of you who live in
Jerusalem, let me explain this to you; listen carefully to what I say*
(Acts 2:14).

Peter was not up alone. The other apostles were with him. And that is
how they continued*: So the word of God spread. The number of disciples
in Jerusalem increased rapidly, and a large number of priests became
obedient to the faith* (Acts 6:7).

The apostles had set the example in preaching the gospel and in winning
the lost in their numbers. Then as they went on great persecution broke
forth: *On that day a great persecution broke out against the church at
Jerusalem, and all except the apostles were scattered throughout Judea
and Samaria.... Those who had been scattered preached the word wherever
they went* (Acts 8:1‒4).

The elders were given to preaching the gospel and winning the lost. The
people were also given to preaching the gospel and winning the lost. Let
us go and do likewise.

### Because the wise over the years have invested their all in this unique task

Some of the most able lives have been poured into winning the lost.
History abounds with such examples. Let us do likewise.

### Because you were won to the Lord by a soul-winner

Remember that someone took the pains to present the gospel to you before
you were saved. You, too, must do the same. Take the pains and present
the gospel to the others so that they, too, may come to the salvation of
our God.

### Because you are a debtor to all men

*I am obligated both to Greeks and non-Greeks, both to the wise and the
foolish* (Romans 1:14). You are a debtor to all men. You owe all men the
gospel of Jesus Christ. You owe Jesus Christ to all men.

I am the wealthiest man on planet Earth. I have what the world needs. I
have the treasure of treasures. I have what presidents and philosophers
do not have. Every believer should walk with his head held high. We have
the final answer. We are the final specialists. That is what we are!

If we want to boast, we will have reasons. We are children of God. We
are co-heirs with Christ. The world owes us nothing. The world has
nothing. We have all in Christ. God has summed up everything in Jesus
and has given us Jesus. We have all in Jesus. We are debtors to all men.
We can pay our debts, and that is by giving them Jesus.

### Because God's purposes in time are tied to those who have been won to him

His purposes are tied to the redeemed. I speak it humbly, but we could
boast. All that God is conceiving to do in time is tied to the redeemed.
As a redeemed man, you are very, very important to God.

God's purposes, in all domains, are tied to the redeemed. We need light
to see this. He works by His Spirit who can only work through the
redeemed. The purposes of God in time are not tied to the world. They
are tied to us. May we know it and act accordingly!

### Because the goal of winning the lost can be attained

The lost can be won. It is not an impossible thing. Therefore, the lost
must be won. If it were impossible to win the lost, you and I might have
an excuse. But now we have none. Let us simply go forth and win them.

### Paul set an example

Paul could say, *even as I try to please everybody in every way. For I
am not seeking my own good but the good of many, so that they may be
saved. Follow my example, as I follow the example of Christ* (1
Corinthians 10:33; 11:1). Go and preach.

Sisters, carry out crusades if you can. Even if they were wrongly
executed for winning the lost, God would forgive you. Do all to win the
lost. Become all that is possible in order that you may win the lost. Do
not let any human condition limit you. If you reach where cockroaches
are eaten, eat them so that you may win the lost there for Christ.

In our ministry we seek to save the largest number in the shortest
possible time. We shall not compromise it for anything.

Because God so loved the world that he gave his son

God so loved the world that He gave His Son Jesus. He, too, so loved the
world that He gave His all. What about you? Can it be said of you that
you so loved the world that you gave your all?

As individuals, and as a ministry, let us invest all that we have, all
that we shall ever have, to save the largest number possible in the
shortest possible time.

## Conclusion

### Your responsibility, my responsibility

I am under duty to God and to man to do everything I can to win souls. I
must work at soul-winning. I must think about soul-winning. I must dream
about soul-winning.

Soul-winning must be my passion. Soul-winning must be my zeal.
Soul-winning must consume me. I yield my all afresh to Christ Who is in
me, so that He may win souls through me.

"I must win souls. It is not enough to just think about it or talk and
pray about it. Put action to your convictions, and you can do that
today. I must do something about it while the fire is burning. Don't let
it go out. Strike it, act on it, and it will blaze brighter" (T. L.
Osborn).

"Personal soul-winning is the call of God which rests upon every
Christian on earth. It is the one dedication which removes the Christian
from the position of a spectator to that of a doer of the Word" (T. L.
Osborn).

I dedicate myself to the [supreme]{.underline} task of world evangelism,
and commit myself to continue to train myself systematically to do my
[utmost]{.underline} for world evangelism.

The saints are to be taught how to win the lost.

The first thing a person is to be taught is how he can be saved.

The second thing he should be taught is how to be holy.

The third thing he should be taught is how to love the Lord Jesus.

The fourth thing he should be taught is how to win the lost to Christ.

Drill every believer in personal soul-winning:

> how to approach new homes,
>
> how to witness,
>
> how to pray for the sick,
>
> how to lead sinners to Christ.

Determine not only to win more souls yourself, but to share with and
inspire other Christians around you to do the same.

### Weeping for the lost: a testimony

"With every block I walked, I was hit again and again with the thought:
'They are lost. They are going to spend eternity without Jesus! Finally,
I tried comforting myself with the thought, But our church has seen
thousands of people converted. Times Square Church is one of the largest
congregations in New York City and we do have a major work of
evangelism. We're reaching some of those lost, hurting people.

But still, something nagged my soul. I had to acknowledge before the
Lord: Oh, Father I don't have the burden I once had! I do not weep the
way I did when I first came to New York City thirty-five years ago. I'm
grateful for all You have done within the four walls of our church. But
in my heart, I know I do not have the same fire!

In 1958, Gwen and I were living in tiny Philipsburg, Pennsylvania, a
town with a population of about 1500 people. In those days, I would walk
into the woods near our home and weep for hours over the souls in New
York City. I owned a little green Chevrolet, and each week as I drove it
to the city to minister, I wept for the entire three-hour drive.
Sometimes I had to pull off to the side of the road, because my heart
was so broken for the lost. I'd stop the engine, walk into the woods,
fall on my face and weep.

After Gwen and I moved our family to Staten Island, I rode by the ferry
to Brooklyn every day to minister and I wept all the way. The other
passengers thought I was crazy. Some even offered me comfort and solace.
But I knew I had the heart of the Lord, and I wept and cried all the
more.

Today I preach in one of the most beautiful theatres in the world, the
historic Mark Hellinger Theatres. I deliver messages to a hungry
congregation, who come from all over the tri-state area to hear sermons
on every possible subject in the Word of God. I love the work of this
ministry, and I thank God for letting me be a part of what He is doing
in New York City.

Yet I wonder how many people in our congregation and how many readers on
our mailing list feel the way I felt walking up Broadway. I had to stop
and ask myself: How long has it been since you wept for the lost? Do you
still have the Lord's burden to reach them with the gospel?"

‒ DAVID WILKERSON,
